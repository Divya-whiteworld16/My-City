GWALIOR

The Historic City

Gwalior is the major city in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh having the population of 2.6 million (approx).

Gwalior is known for its beautiful and historic Fort, which is build in 10 century.Gwalior Fort was also called pearl necklace of the India by Mughal Emperor, Babur.

Beside this there are many other beautiful places in Gwalior. Jai vilas palace , Tansen Museum, Musical Fountain ,Sun Temple and many more. To praise the heavenliness of Gwalior please watch the Gwalior Majestic.To explore more about Gwalior, please visit this website.
